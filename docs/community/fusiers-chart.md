# fUSIers Chart

These resources are designed to facilitate communication among users of functional ultrasound imaging (fUSI) setups. As a community, we kindly ask all users to follow these guidelines to maintain a supportive and respectful environment for everyone.

We encourage open expression across all stages of academic careers but ask that discussions be conducted with care and consideration, avoiding any harm to others' feelings or beliefs. Our goal is to foster meaningful debates that remain grounded in scientific discourse, free from dogma, and within an open-minded framework.

In the spirit of sharing knowledge, data, and code, we ask you to respect the intellectual property of your collaborators and fellow fUSIers by properly crediting all relevant parties when posting on this platform or utilizing its content.

We value collaboration over competition and sincerely thank you for contributing to this collective effort. We hope this repository will support and enhance your daily research endeavors.
